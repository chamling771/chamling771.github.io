Our Story

Welcome to Himalayan Thali, where we bring the authentic flavors of Nepal to the beautiful city of Thimphu, Bhutan.

Founded in 2023 by [Your Name], our restaurant was born out of a passion for sharing the rich culinary traditions of Nepal with our Bhutanese neighbors.

Having grown up in [Your Hometown] Nepal, I learned the art of Nepali cooking from my mother and grandmother. After moving to Bhutan and noticing the lack of authentic Nepali cuisine, I decided to bring these cherished family recipes to Thimphu.

Every dish at Himalayan Thali is prepared with the same love and care as it would be in a Nepali home. We use traditional cooking methods and source the freshest ingredients to ensure an authentic experience.

Our mission is to create a bridge between Nepali and Bhutanese cultures through food, fostering understanding and appreciation between our two Himalayan nations.
